Welcome to Dragon Phoenix Acupuncture

Dragon Phoenix Acupuncture is a premier Traditional Chinese Medicine clinic located in Kissimmee, Florida. We specialize in acupuncture and herbal medicine to help restore balance and promote natural healing in the body.

Our licensed acupuncturists have years of experience treating a wide range of conditions including chronic pain, stress, anxiety, fertility issues, and more.
